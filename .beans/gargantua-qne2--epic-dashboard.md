The landing screen — first impression determines whether users trust the app. Health score gauge as the anchor, reclaimable space and actionable alerts orbiting around it.

## Vision
The dashboard answers "how is my Mac doing?" immediately. Health score is the anchor. Alerts are actionable, not informational. Quick Scan gets users into the core flow fast.

## Features
- Health score gauge (0-100) from CPU, memory, disk, temp
- Actionable alerts with reclaimable space by category
- System info bar (hardware, engine status, MCP status)
- Quick Scan button triggering active profile

## Success Criteria
- [ ] Health score gauge renders with real system metrics
- [ ] Alerts show specific reclaimable amounts with click-through
- [ ] Quick Scan triggers active profile and shows progress
- [ ] Healthy state (95-100) feels like success, not emptiness
