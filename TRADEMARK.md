## The short version

- **The code is free.** Under AGPL-3.0 you may use, study, modify, redistribute,

  corresponding source, keep it AGPL, preserve notices).
- **The brand is not part of that grant.** If you distribute a **modified**
  build, you must **rebrand** it. You may not ship your changes under the

## What you may do without asking

- Run, modify, and redistribute the code under the terms of AGPL-3.0.
- Redistribute our **unmodified** official builds or source while keeping the
  original branding intact (for example, mirroring the release DMG), as long as
  you do not present yourself as the source of the software.
- Refer to Gargantua by name to describe it accurately: "based on Gargantua",
  "a fork of Gargantua", "compatible with Gargantua", in articles, reviews,
  tutorials, talks, and comparisons.
- Use the name or logo to link to the official project.

This is ordinary nominative use. Describing the project truthfully is always
fine.

## What requires rebranding (or our written permission)

- Distributing a **modified** version of Gargantua under the name "Gargantua"
  or with the Gargantua icon, wordmark, or logo.
- Using "Gargantua" or "Inceptyon Labs" in the name of your product, company,
  domain, app-store listing, or social account in a way that implies it is the
  official project or that we are affiliated with or endorse it.
- Using the Gargantua icon or logo as the icon or logo of your own application.
- Any use that implies sponsorship, endorsement, or affiliation that does not
  exist.

## How to rebrand a fork

If you distribute a modified build, please:

1. Choose a distinct name that is not "Gargantua" and is not confusingly
   similar, and use a matching bundle identifier (not `com.inceptyon.gargantua`
   and not anything under `com.inceptyon.*`).
2. Replace the application icon and remove the Gargantua wordmark, logo, and
   other brand assets.
3. Remove or replace references to Inceptyon Labs, the Polar.sh storefront,
   license keys and activation, and the official update (Sparkle appcast) URLs.
4. Sign and notarize under your own Apple Developer ID.
5. Make clear that your build is an independent fork and not the official
   Gargantua.

You may still state factually that your project is based on or forked from
Gargantua.
